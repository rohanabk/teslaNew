#1.Rules

1) A team should be of two members compulsorily.
2) There will be two phases.
3) 1st phase will be written type with 30 MCQ questions carrying 1 mark each (elimination round).
4) In 30 questions there will be 5star questions which will be considered in case of tie.
5) For 2nd phase 5 top teams will be selected and the phase will be verbal phase.
6) There will be 3 rounds in the 2nd phase
  • a) Round 1-> in which 2 questions for each team carrying 5 mark each will be asked; bonus will carry 2 marks in case of passing of question (elimination round-top 3 are selected)
  • b) Round 2-> A team member of each team will be given a name of technical word or element and the team member has to make other member understand the given word or element (without saying a single word and writing the word) by drawing on the board (non-elimination round-but carries marks).
  • C) Round 3-> Rapid fire round: 5 questions will be asked from each team continuously for 1 mark each There will be no passing of question to other team in this round. The top 3 teams which consume less times will be rewarded with 3, 2,1 marks respectively.
7) Any kind of cheating or malpractice will directly lead to disqualification of the team from entire event 8. Using of electronic devices is strictly prohibited.
