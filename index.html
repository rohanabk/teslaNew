Tesla is flagship event of Electrical and Electronics department of K.L.S Gogte Institute of Technology. The name Tesla was given based on Nikola Tesla , a famous inventor who is best known for his contributions to the design of the modern alternating current (AC) electricity supply system. The main intention of this event is to provide a platform whereby students could present their ideas and the technical knowledge based on mainstream scientific works in the field of electrical engineering. This year Tesla is being conducted on 8th and 9th March, 2019. It will be an intercollege level event and the students only from all electrical and elctronics enginnering departments are allowed to take part.
